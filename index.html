About The Game

Your Journey to Football Glory

Soccer Career 2025 is a player-focused football career simulator. You control one player from the start of their journey to the peak of the world stage. Train, manage your energy, play matches, earn transfers, and represent your country. Every decision you make shapes your legacy in the beautiful game.
